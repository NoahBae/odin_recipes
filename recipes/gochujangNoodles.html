Gochujang Noodles (고추장 냉면)

Behind The Noodles

Gochujang noodles are a staple in the Korean household and is a dish that is not only feasible to make at home BUT also pretty easy to make to give it a go.

Ingredients

  • Scallions / Green Onion
  • Momofuku Noodles/Udon noodles of choice (I prefer azami udon noodles)
  • Gochujang
  • Cooking Sake
  • White Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Any choice of meat / veggie protein
  • Chili Crunch Oil - optional

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Mix wet ingredients into a cup: (1/2) tbsp gochujang, (1/4) cup of cooking sake, 2 stalks of green onion/scallions
  2. Cook choice of meat/protein with (1/2) tbsp of cooking oil of choice
  3. Bring a pot to a rolling boil and place udon noodles / momofuku noodles of choice into pot
  5. Cook noodles until texture desired is reached and drain. Leave noodles out to dry for 3-5 minutes
  6. Once dried, combine udon/momofuku noodles with wet ingredient mixture (cooking sake, gochujang, etc.).
  7. Stir fry on medium-low heat for several minutes with chili crunch oil combined into mixture if desired.
  8. Toss in protein with the noodles as soon as sake has dissolved and serve!
