Ube Yogurt

Behind The Ube

A fairly simple, high protein recipe that's bound to get you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • Fage Yogurt / GAssorted reek Yogurt
  • Butterfly Ube Extract
  • Sweetener / add to taste
  • fruits if desired
  • coconut flakes

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Scoop (1/2-1 cup) of fage yogurt and serve to a mixing bowl
  2. Combine yogurt with (1/4-1/2 tsp) of butterfly ube extract
  3. Mix thoroughly until ube extract concentrate has disappeared - you'll know this happens when there are no more streaks of purple bowl and yogurt is of a completely purple hue.
  4. Add in sweetener of choice, mix until completely gone, add to taste if more is desired
  5. Stir in fruits if desired, coconut flakes also to enhance flavor, and enjoy!
